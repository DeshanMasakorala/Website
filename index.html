SRI LANKA

Overview of Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, formerly known as Ceylon, is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean, just off the southeastern coast of India. It covers an area of approximately 65,610 square kilometers and has a population of about 22 million people, making it one of the most densely populated countries in the world.

Places to Visit

Sri Lanka boasts numerous attractions -

  • Anuradhapura: Ancient capital known for its well-preserved ruins.
  • Sigiriya: Famous for its rock fortress and frescoes.
  • Kandy: Home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic.
  • Galle: A historic fortified city with colonial architecture.
  • Yala National Park: Renowned for its wildlife and leopard population.

These sites attract both tourists and pilgrims alike.
